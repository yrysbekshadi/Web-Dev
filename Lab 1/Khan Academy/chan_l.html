ü•û Fluffy Pancakes

Source: Wikipedia

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1

Details

  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: 2

Steps

  1. Mix flour, milk, and egg in a bowl.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form.
  4. Flip and cook until golden.

üçù Creamy Pasta

Source: Wikipedia

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Pasta 200 g
Cream 100 ml
Cheese 50 g

Details

  • Prep time: 5 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 2

Steps

Follow these simple steps:

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Heat cream in a pan.
  3. Add cheese and stir.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce and serve.
